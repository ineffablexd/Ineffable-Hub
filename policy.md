# Privacy Policy – Ineffable Hub

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Ineffable Hub ("the App") is developed and maintained by Ineffable. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Ineffable Hub application.

## 1. Information Collection

Ineffable Hub does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information from users.

The app operates primarily offline and does not require users to create an account or provide personal data.

## 2. Permissions

The app may request certain device permissions in order to provide its features:

**Storage / Media Access**
Used only to allow users to select images or media files from their device when using customization features such as wallpapers, icon generation, or mockup creation.

**Internet Access**
Used only for loading wallpapers, updates, or other app resources when required.

These permissions are used strictly for app functionality and not for collecting personal data.

## 3. User Content

Some features allow users to select images, wallpapers, or media files from their device.

These files remain on the user's device and are not uploaded, collected, or stored by Ineffable Hub.

## 4. Children's Privacy

Ineffable Hub is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
